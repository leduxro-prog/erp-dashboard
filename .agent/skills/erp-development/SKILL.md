# Best Skills for Building/Implementing ERP Systems

ERP skills focus on managing complex, integrated systems that handle core business operations.

## Key Competencies

### Technical & Functional Configuration
- **Mastery**: SAP, Oracle, NetSuite (and internal systems like CYPHER).
- **Actions**:
  - Configure systems to match business needs.
  - Customize modules for specific workflows.

### Business Process Optimization
- **Goal**: Efficiency.
- **Actions**:
  - Analyze current workflows to identify bottlenecks.
  - Redesign processes before implementation.

### Data Migration & Integrity
- **Challenge**: Moving legacy data.
- **Actions**:
  - Securely transfer data.
  - Validate data integrity post-migration.
  - Handle schema mapping and transformation.

### Project Management
- **Scope**: Organizing, planning, and executing deployment.
- **Actions**:
  - Manage budgets and timelines.
  - Coordinate cross-functional teams.

### Change Management & Training
- **Goal**: Adoption.
- **Actions**:
  - Facilitate adoption across departments.
  - Develop training materials for end-users.

## Soft Skills Required
- **Project Management**: Organizing timelines.
- **Change Management**: Facilitating adoption.
