# Internal Communications Skill Summary

This skill helps you craft internal company communications using standardized formats. Here's what it covers:

**Communication Types Supported:**
The skill handles "3P updates (Progress, Plans, Problems) team updates," "company-wide newsletters," FAQ responses, status reports, leadership updates, project updates, and incident reports.

**How It Works:**
The process involves three steps: first, identify what type of communication you need; second, locate the matching guideline from the examples directory; and third, follow the formatting and tone instructions provided in that specific file.
